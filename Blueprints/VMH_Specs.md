## 1. PHYSICAL MEDIUM AND 5D STORAGE

### 1.1. Material Specification
* **Hardware:** Photorefractive crystal (e.g., Stoichiometric Lithium Niobate - $\text{LiNbO}_3$, or High-Purity Fused Quartz for resilience).
* **Storage Mechanism:** **Bragg Gratings.** Knowledge is recorded by intersecting two laser beams within the crystal volume, creating permanent, volumetric changes in the refractive index.

### 1.2. The 5D-Memory Concept
In the Simureality framework, a vector must be defined by more than just three spatial coordinates.
* **Dimensions:** Data is stored using **5 Degrees of Freedom:**
    1.  X, Y, Z (Spatial Location within the crystal volume).
    2.  $\theta$ (Angle of the reference beam).
    3.  $\lambda$ (Wavelength/Color of the reference beam).
* **Result:** Extreme storage density (Terabytes per cubic centimeter) and the ability to retrieve data in a non-linear, associative manner.
