## 2. ARCHITECTURE: NON-LINEAR RESONANCE CHAMBER

### 2.1. Mechanism: Modified Mach-Zehnder Interferometer (MZI)
The Trizistor is realized as a **triple-input interferometer** integrated into the Photonic Integrated Circuit (PIC).

### 2.2. The Non-Linear Medium
The critical component is the **Resonance Chamber** where the three beams intersect.
* **Material:** The chamber is filled or lined with a **Non-Linear Optical Material** (e.g., specific polymers or quantum dots).
* **Function:** This material ensures that the energy output amplifies **exponentially** (non-linearly) when all three vectors are in perfect phase and amplitude consensus. This amplification is the source of the "Flash of Insight" (Logical 1).

### 2.3. Anti-Entropy Logic
* **Dissonance (Noise):** If even one vector (e.g., $V_A$) is out of phase, the destructive interference dominates. The non-linear amplification is broken, and the output is instantly **cancelled** (Logical 0 / Darkness).
* **Advantage:** This ensures the Trizistor serves as a **Physical Filter of Truth**, consuming zero energy on irrelevant or mismatched data, aligning with the $\Sigma K \to \min$ principle.
